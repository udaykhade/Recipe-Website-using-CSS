Aam Panna

Ingredients

  • Raw mango- 2
  • Sugar- ½ cup
  • Cardamom powder- ¼ teaspoon
  • Saffron strands-1/4 teaspoon
  • Water- 5 cups

Recipe

  1. Dice the mangoes and mix with sugar and water.
  2. Boil mango till soft.
  3. Cool it and blend in a mixer.
  4. Mix cardamom powder and saffron strands and stir on low flame.
  5. Chill and serve.
